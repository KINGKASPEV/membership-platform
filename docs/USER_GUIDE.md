# User Guide: Member Registration

Welcome to the National Membership Registration & Validation Platform. This guide will walk you through the process of registering as a member.

## Getting Started
Navigate to the registration portal homepage. You will see a "Start Registration" button.

## Step 1: Personal Information
Enter your personal details accurately.
- **Name**: First, Last, and Middle name (optional).
- **Contact**: Email (optional) and Phone Number (required).
- **Demographics**: Date of Birth and Gender.

**Note**: All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.

## Step 2: Location
Identify where you reside/worship.
1. Select your **State** of Origin/Residence.
2. Select your **LGA** (Local Government Area).
3. Select your **Ward**.
4. Select your **Parish**. This is the parish where your registration will be validated.

## Step 3: Denomination
- Indicate if you are **Catholic**.
- If NO, verify if you are a "Friend of Catholics" or associated denomination? (Select from list).
- Indicate if you have a **PVC** (Permanent Voters Card).

## Step 4: Review & Submit
Review all information on the summary screen.
- Click **Edit** next to any section to make changes.
- Click **Submit Registration** to finalize.

## What Happens Next?
Your registration will be sent to the Parish Admin of the parish you selected. Once they validate your details, you will be officially registered. You may be contacted by the parish for verification if needed.
